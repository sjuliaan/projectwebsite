My Beat

Welcome to my personal music collection! Here you'll find the songs and artists that define my taste in music. From smooth R&B grooves to alternative indie anthems and heartfelt OPM tracks, this is the soundtrack of my life. Each selection holds special meaning to me - whether it's the lyrics that resonate, the melodies that move me, or the memories they evoke. Take a listen and maybe you'll discover your next favorite song!

R&B

Disillusioned

Artist: Daniel Caesar

Album: Never Enough


Never Enough is the third studio album by Canadian singer-songwriter Daniel Caesar, released on April 7, 2023. The album blends R&B, soul, and alternative influences, featuring introspective lyrics about love, self-reflection, and personal growth.

Thinkin Bout You

Artist: Frank Ocean

Album: Channel Orange


Channel Orange is the debut studio album by Frank Ocean, released on July 10, 2012. The album blends R&B, soul, funk, and psychedelic influences, featuring vivid storytelling about love, wealth, identity, and longing.

My Boo

Artist: Usher

Album: Confessions


Confessions is Usher’s fourth studio album, released on March 23, 2004. A defining R&B record, it explores love, infidelity, and redemption, featuring massive hits like Yeah! and My Boo.


Alternative/Indie

The Only Exception

Artist: Paramore

Album: Brand New Eyes


Brand New Eye is Paramore's third studio album, released on September 29, 2009. A mix of alternative rock and emo, it delves into themes of love, doubt, and personal struggles.

Dear Maria, Count Me In

Artist: All Time Low

Album: So Wrong, It's Right


So Wrong, It's Right is All Time Low's second studio album, released on September 25, 2007. A pop-punk staple filled with energetic hooks and youthful anthems about love and rebellion.

I Wanna Be Yours

Artist: Arctic Monkeys

Album: AM


AM is Arctic Monkeys' fifth studio album, released on September 9, 2013. A moody, groove-heavy rock album blending indie, R&B, and hip-hop influences, exploring love, desire, and heartbreak.
