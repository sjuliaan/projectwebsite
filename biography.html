From Curiosity to Code: My Tech Journey

Childhood Years | Teenage Years | College Years

Childhood Years

      1 I am Seth Juian B. Cammayo, born in Ilagan, Isabela, where life was simple and full of adventure. 2 As a child, I spent most of my days playing outside with friends, exploring the streets, and riding bicycles until sunset. 3 My parents encouraged my curiosity, which led to a deep interest in technology. 4 I remember dismantling toys to see how they worked, often struggling to put them back together. 5 My early education was filled with excitement as I eagerly absorbed new knowledge. 6 While I didn’t particularly enjoy mathematics and science, a computer subject fueled my passion for computers. 7 I was fascinated by how a single click could perform tasks. 8 Family gatherings were lively, filled with laughter, delicious food, and storytelling. 9 Every summer, we visited my grandparents in the province, where I experienced a slower, more peaceful way of life. 10 Those childhood moments shaped my values and taught me the importance of family, hard work, and perseverance. 11 My love for technology started when I began playing PlayStation, fueling my curiosity about how games were created. 12 This curiosity led me to explore computers, and I would often visit our computer shop just to play. 13 I spent hours experimenting with programs, sometimes breaking them and asking for help to fix them. 14 My parents noticed my passion and allowed me to explore computers freely. 15 Eventually, I became comfortable navigating systems and learning how applications worked. 16 My fascination grew stronger when I discovered the internet and its endless possibilities. 17 Even as a child, I knew I wanted to be involved in something related to computers. 18 The joy of learning something new and applying it to real-life situations became my motivation. 19 Technology felt like an infinite world waiting to be explored, and I was motivated to dive into it. 20 Looking back, those formative years set the foundation for my journey into technology and shaped my ambition for the future.

Teenage Years

balancing academics, extracurricular activities, and social life. 3 I became more involved in school clubs, particularly those related to technology. 4 It was during this time that I started fixing and troubleshooting computers for friends and family. 5 I enjoyed solving problems, whether it was a slow system, a virus, or a hardware issue. 6 I also developed an interest in coding, but due to my busy high school life, I never really started learning it. 7 I grew an interest in hacking, often watching YouTubers who taught how to hack games. 8 High school introduced me to the importance of teamwork and leadership, as I took on roles in group projects and competitions. 9 I joined activities that pushed me to collaborate and enhance my problem-solving skills. 10 Friendships played a crucial role during this stage, and I built strong connections with people who shared my interests. 11 Late-night study sessions, group projects, and school events became unforgettable memories. 12 At times, academic pressure felt overwhelming, especially during exam weeks. 13 I learned to manage my time and prioritize my goals, ensuring I stayed on track. 14 I faced moments of self-doubt, questioning whether I was truly capable of pursuing a career in technology. 15 However, my teachers and mentors guided me in refining my skills and encouraged me to pursue Information Technology. 16 They saw my passion and reassured me that I had the potential to excel in the field. 17 By the time I graduated, I had a clear vision of what I wanted to achieve in college. 18 High school was a challenging yet transformative period that helped me build the confidence and skills I needed for the next phase of my life. 19 Teenage years were a rollercoaster of emotions, preparing me for the next big step—college. 20 I discovered my passion for technology during this time, spending hours experimenting with computers. These experiences fueled my desire to pursue a career in IT.

College Years

more about programming, database management, and system development. 3 The transition from high school to college was overwhelming at first, as I had to adjust to new schedules, teaching styles, and academic expectations. 4 I gradually adapted to the new environment and developed better study habits. 5 I learned to be more independent, responsible, and resourceful in handling academic requirements. 6 Group projects became more complex, requiring collaboration, patience, and problem-solving skills. 7 My passion for computers continued to grow as I worked on various activities and projects assigned by our professors. 8 I found joy in building systems, troubleshooting errors, and understanding the logic behind different algorithms. 9 There were moments of frustration, especially when debugging codes or meeting deadlines, but the sense of accomplishment made all the effort worth it. 10 I realized that mistakes were part of the learning process and that perseverance was the key to improvement. 11 College life also provided opportunities to meet new friends who were equally passionate about technology and academic excellence. 12 Surrounding myself with like-minded individuals motivated me to push harder and expand my knowledge beyond the classroom. 13 I faced challenges, including difficult subjects and personal struggles, but I persevered through them with determination. 14 Balancing academics and personal life was not easy, yet I managed to stay focused on my goals. 15 The hands-on experiences, such as activities and projects, have given me valuable insights into the real-world applications of technology. 16 Every challenge I encountered became a stepping stone toward growth, preparing me for my future career. 17 I am excited about what lies ahead and the opportunities that await me in the field of Information Technology. 18 As I move forward, I am excited to apply everything I have learned and continue growing in my field. 19 The experiences and lessons I have gained and will continue to gain throughout my journey will help shape me into a new person, ready to take on the challenges of the real world. 20 College has been a transformative experience, reinforcing my passion for technology and solidifying my dreams for the future.
